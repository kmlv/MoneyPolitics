Choose your preferred system of tax rates. Remember that as the level increases, the tax payment of the players with the highest income is going to increase and the one of the players with the lowest will decrease

The tax rates are going to be applied to the income before sending the messages.

The following table contains the average tax rates for each of the possible tax levels. Click on your preferred system and on the "next" button to move forward.

Base Income Average Tax Rates by Tax System
9 0.4 0.3 0.2 0.1 0
15 0.4 0.35 0.3 0.2 0
25 0.4 0.375 0.35 0.3 0.15
40 0.4 0.4 0.4 0.4 0.6
80 0.4 0.45 0.55 0.65 0.8
125 0.4 0.55 0.65 0.8 0.9

The median of all the selected systems is going to be chosen. Example: Suppose you have chosen System 1. The other 8 members of your group have chosen systems: 1, 4, 5, 2, 5, 4, 5, 2. Sorted from low to high we have
1: 1
2: 1
3: 2
4: 2
5: 4 (= median system)
6: 4
7: 5
8: 5
9: 5

The median in the example would be 4. Therefore, this is the tax system that is going to be applied to the income of the whole group.
